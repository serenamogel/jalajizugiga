Their parents

Echikunwoke was born in Spokane, Washington on 28. May 1983 to Anita Laurie, a mother of English, Irish and German descent. Her father, Onigwe Versato Echikunwoke, is Nigerian and belongs to the Igbo tribe. Echikunwoke was born in Spokane and raised in Chinle, Arizona on a Navajo Indian reservation.

Her grandfather is a traditional Igbo chief, which makes her an African allegiance. Megalyn is not her parents’ only child, she has two siblings – a brother named Miki and a sister named Misty.
