Tiwa Savage Reveals How She Got Blackmailed Over A $3x Tape Video

Songstress Tiwa Savage has revealed how she got blackmailed over a $3x tape but was so strong not to give in to the demands of the blackmailer.

Speaking in an interview, Tiwa Savage disclosed that someone sent her a video of herself and the man she’s dating at the moment in bed trying to blackmail her and demanding money but was strong and firm enough not to give in.

According to her, everyone around her was going crazy including the man she is dating but it occurred to her what happens if she pays the demand of the blackmailer and he/she comes back after some time for another money or even release it after taking the money.

Therefore, she stood her ground and said to herself that she won’t allow anyone to blackmail her for doing something that is natural with a man she’s in love with and that was how she never gave the blackmailer what he/she wanted from her.
