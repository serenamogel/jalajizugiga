Gims biography

Maitre Gims is a rapper and musician from the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Maitre Gims was born in May 1986 in Kinshasa, DR Congo. Before going solo, he was a part of the hip-hop crew Sexion d’Asaut. L’Apogee, their best-known album, was published in 2012 and peaked at number one in France and Belgium.

Subliminal, the first solo studio album by Gims, debuted at #1 in Belgium and #2 in France in 2013. 2015 saw the release of his second album, Mon coeur avait raison, which debuted at No. 1 in both countries. His 2018 solo album Ceinture noire, which sold almost 900,000 copies, peaked at #1 in France.

He has worked with many well-known musicians from throughout the world over his career, including Pitbull, Lil Wayne, Stromae, Maluma, and Sting. In addition to this, he founded his own record label, “Monstre Marin Corporation,” published his own comic book, “Au coeur du vortex,” and started his own apparel line, “Vortex VX.”
