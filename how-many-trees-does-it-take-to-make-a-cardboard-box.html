Is cardboard made of tree?

Fast-growing pine trees provide the primary raw material used to make corrugated cardboard. … After the trees are harvested, they are stripped of their limbs and the trunks are prepared to be shipped by truck to a pulp mill. At the pulp mill, pulping and processing is used to break down wood chips into fibrous pulp.
