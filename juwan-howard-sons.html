Who is Juwan Howard? 

Juwan Howard born Juwan Antonio Howard is an Ex American basketball player and the current coach of his alma mater, the University of Michigan.

He has won two NBA Championships with Miami Heat and has also played for other NBA teams such as Washington Bullets, Dallas Mavericks, Denver Nuggets, Orlando Magic, Houston Rockets, Charlotte Bobcats, and Portland Trail Blazers.

He retired as a basket player in 2013 at Miami Heat and served them as an assistant coach for over five seasons before becoming the head coach at Michigan in 2019.
