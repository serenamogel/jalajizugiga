AEW Fight For The Fallen Streaming For Free

AEW Fight For The Fallen is the company’s third event and the second one that won’t cost American and Canadian fans anything to watch. AEW Fyter Fest was free to stream on B/R Live and it will be once again on July 13th.

This is a pretty big announcement and really makes us wonder if it could become a trend for the streaming events on B/R Live to be free except for their tent pole pay-per-view shows. So far Double Or Nothing and All Out are the only actual pay-per-view events AEW has, but more could eventually be lined up.

AEW’s Fight for the Fallen is available for pre-order on FITE.TV for fans outside of U.S and Canada. The cost will be $12.99 or £8.99 in the UK. That still isn’t a substantial amount and should help draw in fans.
