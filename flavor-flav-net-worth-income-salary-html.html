Flavor Flav is the stage name of William Jonathan Drayton Jr., who is a well-known American rap artist, singer, television personality, actor, and voice actor who is best known for his work on the hit show The Game. Flavor Flav is probably best known to the general public as a member of the famous hip hop group “Public Enemy,” which he founded. The group, which comprises of Chuck D, Flav, DJ Lord, Professor Griff, The S1W, and Khari Wynn, was formed in New York in 1982 by Chuck D, Flav, DJ Lord, Professor Griff, and Khari Wynn. When “Public Enemy” released their first studio album, “Yo! Bum Rush the Show,” which produced two songs and reached at #125 on the Billboard Top LPs list, they became a household name in the music industry. The album has received widespread critical praise and has been named one of the 100 Best Rap Albums of all time by Rolling Stone magazine. It was also included in the magazine’s list of the 500 Greatest Albums of All Time.
