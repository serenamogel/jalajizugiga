Valerie Simpson Net Worth

Valerie Simpson is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer born on August 26, 1946 in The Bronx, NY. She is best known for her collaborations with Nickolas Ashford, including the classic hits "Ain't No Mountain High Enough", "You're All I Need to Get By", and "I'm Every Woman".

About

Collaborated on Ain't No Mountain High Enough, You're All I Need to Get By, and I'm Every Woman with Nickolas Ashford.

Before Fame

She played piano and sung with New York City's White Rock Baptist Church's choir where she met Ashford.

Trivia

She and Ashford were inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame in 2002.

Family Life

She and Ashford were married in 1974. They had two daughters together.

Associated With

She, Ashford, and former Ikettes member Joshie Jo Armstead wrote Let's Go Get Stoned, which Ray Charles turned into a #1 1966 U.S. R&B hit.
