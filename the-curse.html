The Curse - Showtime - Watch on Paramount Plus

SHOWTIME® Original series THE CURSE centers on Whitney and Asher Siegel, a newlywed couple struggling to bring their vision for eco-conscious housing to the small community of Española, New Mexico. But, their efforts are complicated when an eccentrically flawed reality TV producer, Dougie, sees opportunity in their story. As the series unfolds, the couple find themselves caught in a mysterious web of ethical and moral gray zones – all while trying to keep their relationship afloat. THE CURSE stars Oscar® winner Emma Stone, Nathan Fielder and Benny Safdie. Premiering November 10 on SHOWTIME — streaming with the Paramount+ with Showtime plan.
