Introduction to R. Riveter and Shark Tank Appearance

R. Riveter is a company that gained national attention after appearing on the hit TV show Shark Tank. Founded by military spouses Lisa Bradley and Cameron Cruse, R. Riveter creates handbags and accessories while providing portable careers for military spouses across the country. Their appearance on Shark Tank was not just a pitch for investment but also a spotlight on their mission to empower a community often affected by the transient nature of military life. In this article, we will delve into the net worth of R. Riveter as of 2024, following their Shark Tank success.

Lisa Bradley and Cameron Cruse, the founders of R. Riveter, started their business out of a garage, driven by the desire to create a social impact. R. Riveter’s Mission and Business Model

R. Riveter’s business model is unique in that it not only focuses on producing quality products but also on providing employment opportunities to military spouses. The company operates a remote manufacturing model where parts of handbags are crafted by military spouses across the country and then assembled into the final product at the company’s headquarters.
