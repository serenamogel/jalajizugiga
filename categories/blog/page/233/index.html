Did Patrick McEnroe ever win Wimbledon?

published on February 28, 2024
Patrick McEnroe  is a former American professional tennis player, broadcaster, and Davis Cup captain. Patrick McEnroe never won Wimbledon. He won one singles title and sixteen doubles titles, including the French Open in 1989. His career high rankings in singles were world No. 28 and world No. 3 in doubles.
