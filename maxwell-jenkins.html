Maxwell Jenkins is an American child star who is blessed with exceptional talent and gifted skills. The young entertainer has marked his own identity at such a young age which is truly an inspiration for youngsters. He has appeared in various television series and films for which he gained a lot of fame. The most talented actor managed to steal the hearts of millions of his fans and followers with his amazing acting prowess.

He was born on 3rd May 2005, USA where he raised and grew up with siblings and parents. He is the son of Jeff Jenkins (father) and Julie Greenberg (mother). His parents are the co-founders and directors of Midnight Circus, which was located in Chicago.

Maxwell Jenkins was a career enthusiast in his childhood and he wanted to be a great actor. When he expressed a wish in front of his parents, they encouraged and motivated him to go with passion.

Career

Before joining the acting line, he performed with the circus and performed tightwire, walking, juggling, and playing mandolin at the age of 3.

In 2013-2014, Maxwell Jenkins started his professional career with the role he played in ABC’s series named “Betrayal”. It was the time when he was just 8 years of age. In the series, he portrayed the role of Oliver which was liked by everyone.

In 2015, Maxwell Jenkins appeared in the 12 episodes of the television series “Sense 8” in which he played the role of a young Will.

In 2015, he also worked in 8 episodes of “NCIS: New Orleans” in which he appeared as Ryan Griggs.

Talking about his film career, he made a debut movie. In 2016, Maxwell Jenkins portrayed the role of Ryan Jensen in “A Family Man” for which he got huge applause from viewers, fans, and followers.

Since 2018, he has been appearing as Will Robinson in “Lost in Space”, the Netflix remake of the “1965 TV series” for which he gained immense love and amazing feedback from viewers.

In 2020, he was last seen in the film named “Good Joe Bell” which is post-production and he played the role of Joseph Bell. Undoubtedly, he won the hearts of millions with breath-taking performance.

Achievement

In 2019, the young, talented actor, Maxwell Jenkins earned a nomination for “Best Supporting Actor” in Streaming Presentation at the Saturn Award ceremony.
